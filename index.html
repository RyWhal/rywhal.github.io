Hello World

Turns out a hello world script is pretty easy in coffee script.

hello.coffee:

hello = "Hello, World"
console.log hello

compiled into js:

$ coffee -p hello.coffee
(function() {
  var hello;

  hello = "Hello, World";

  console.log(hello);

}).call(this);

and running it:

$ node hello.js
Hello, World
